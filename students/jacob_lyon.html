Biography

Sommelier-certified. Exemplary baker. Amateur photographer. Jacob (UVA 2012) — from Storm Lake, Iowa — is interested in building things and drinking lots of wine this summer. While at University of Virginia, Jacob double majored in music and psychology. At college, he sang a cappella, was part of a student theater group, and a leader in the class council. After graduating, Jacob spent a year in a vineyard in Charlottes, Virginia – the same location as the UVA campus – and then later moved to New York to work at ZeroCater, a startup solving catering logistics. Jacob, while doing all this, managed to get himself a sommelier certification, and insists that his favorite wine always depends on the day and his mood. Even if you have favorites, start with those and then branch out, he recommends. Jacob is also a huge foodie and generous baker. Jacob is at Flatiron so that he can build things and make the world better. More specifically, he wouldn’t mind a product management gig after the bootcamp so that he can talk to customers, decide what to do, and when to build.
